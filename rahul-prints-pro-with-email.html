Important: Rahul Prints only confirms the order after the Razorpay payment is verified on the server.
  1. Tap the Razorpay button to open secure checkout.
  2. Complete the payment using UPI, card, wallet, or net banking.
  3. Stay on this page while we verify the payment and generate the receipt automatically.
If the payment window closes or the bank declines the payment Rahul Prints does not mark the order as paid until Razorpay returns a successful verified payment. If the payment fails, simply retry from this page and do not leave until the receipt appears here.
